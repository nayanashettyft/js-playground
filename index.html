Bharatnatyam, a classical dance form from south India

Welcome!!

The word Bharatnatyam is formed of 4 sanskrit words, Bha-ra-ta-natyam,

  • Bha stands for Bhava
  • Ra stands for Raga
  • Ta stands for tala
  • Natyam

Bhava meaning emotions, Raga meaning music, Tala meaning rhythm and Natya meaning dance. In this dance form, dancers express a scene with a combination of facial expressions(nritya) and pure dance(nritta).
Conventionally this is a solo dance performed only by women in the Hindu temples in south India however these days there are a lot of different variants of this dance performed all over the world.
